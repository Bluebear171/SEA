Binary Analysis

BitBlaze: Binary Analysis for Computer Security (pdf, download)

The BitBlaze project aims to design and develop a powerful binary analysis platform and employ the platform in order to (1) analyze and develop novel COTS protection and diagnostic mechanisms and (2) analyze, understand, and develop defenses against malicious code.

BAP: The Next-Generation Binary Analysis Platform (pdf, download)

A good candidate for an intermediate representation language to add support in SEA. We are starting to support this intermediate language to lift binaries into SEA framework.
